  • 'Unbalanced Forces' Installation at Lábor gallery

    Newest creation of Jérôme Li-Thiao-Té is exhibited at Lábor gallery. Aren’t we making the transition from former to new times, the scapegoat for our lack of intervention and responsible actions?
